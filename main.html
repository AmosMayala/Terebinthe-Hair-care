Welcome to Terebinthe

Térébinthe is a natural hair care brand creating herbal oils and pomades for coily hair. Inspired by the biblical terebinth tree,a symbol of strength and flourishing, much like Abraham under whom the tree stood. Térébinthe is more than just hair care. Customers love our products, and we already supply one salon and sell online. Our mission is to empower Black women to embrace their natural hair with confidence while nourishing both hair and self-esteem. We create herbal, effective, and affordable products that celebrate natural beauty and encourage self-love. I am seeking funding to purchase containers, labels, equipment, and ingredients to grow production, supply more salons, and continue helping women grow, shine, and thrive in the hair (and life) they were made for.

VISION

To empower Black women to embrace their natural hair with confidence by providing herbal, nourishing, and affordable hair care products that promote healthy hair and self-love

MISSION STATEMENT

To create high-quality, herbal hair care products specifically designed for coily hair, promoting healthy hair growth and self-love.

TARGET CUSTOMERS

Térébinthe is designed for women with natural or coily hair, as well as salons that specialise in natural hair care. It also appeals to customers seeking affordable, handmade, herbal alternatives to mass- produced hair products.

What Makes It Different

Unlike many commercial brands that use synthetic fillers, Térébinthe products are crafted in small batches with highly natural, herb based ingredients. This ensures quality, potency, and a personal touch that customers can trust.

FINAL THOUGHTS

Térébinthe began simply because I couldn’t afford the fancy hair products I wanted, so I decided to make something for my sister and me. What started as a small, personal solution has grown into a brand dedicated to helping Black women embrace their natural hair with confidence. With the right support, we can expand production, reach more salons and online customers, and continue creating effective, affordable, and empowering hair care. Térébinthe is more than products, it’s about self-love, growth, and flourishing, just like the terebinth tree that inspired our name.

Our Products

At Térébinthe, we specialize in natural, herbal based hair care products that support healthy hair growth and scalp care. Our mission is to help women embrace their natural hair with confidence by providing safe, effective, and affordable solutions. Our range includes the Herbal Hair Pomade/Vitamin, our most popular product, loved for its ability to lock in moisture, stimulate growth, and nourish the scalp. We also offer the Herbal Hair Oil, a lightweight oil perfect for hydrating and refreshing hair between wash days. These products meet our clients’ biggest needs, moisture retention and growth.

Herbal Hair Pomade/Vitamin

Our herbal hair vitamins deeply nourish the hair strands, stimulate hair growth, and improve hair health. They strengthen strands, lock in moisture, and help prevent breakage, making them ideal for those seeking longer, healthier, and more resilient hair

Herbal Hair Oil

Our herbal hair oil is designed to nourish, protect, and restore hair. It improves scalp circulation, prevents dryness, and enhances shine while supporting healthy, consistent growth. Regular use strengthens hair and reduces breakage,prevents dryness scalp and dandruff.

Our Ingredients

HERBAL HAIR VITAMIN

Our Herbal Hair Vitamins are designed to boost growth and restore hair health with a nutrient-packed formula. Fenugreek and Rosemary stimulate blood flow to the scalp, encouraging faster, thicker growth. Hibiscus and Moringa are rich in Vitamin A and Vitamin C, which strengthen roots and protect hair from damage, while Cloves and Eucalyptus keep the scalp fresh and flake- free. Castor Oil, naturally rich in Vitamin E, seals in moisture for softer, fuller hair. Set in a Pomade Base of glycerine and petroleum, this blend deeply hydrates and shields hair from dryness. Ideal for damaged, thinning, or brittle hair, it reduces breakage, soothes itchy scalps, and supports strong, healthy growth with consistent use.

HERBAL HAIR Oil

Our Herbal Hair Oil delivers concentrated nutrients directly to the scalp. Cloves and Cinnamon stimulate circulation for healthy growth, while Hibiscus provides Vitamin C to boost collagen production and strengthen strands. Moringa adds Vitamin A and Vitamin C to nourish hair follicles, and Citronnelle (Lemongrass) Oil balances scalp oils to reduce irritation. Castor Oil and the Carrier Oil base supply Vitamin E to deeply moisturize and protect hair. Perfect for regular scalp massages or hot-oil treatments, this oil reduces breakage, restores shine, and leaves hair hydrated and more manageable. It’s ideal for anyone wanting stronger roots, a healthier scalp, and visible length retention.
